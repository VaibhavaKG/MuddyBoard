ℹ️ MuddyBoard Info & Guide

🌟 About MuddyBoard

MuddyBoard is a premium, interactive sensory playground designed specifically for toddlers aged 1–4 years. It is completely ad-free, runs offline, requires no backend login, and provides a safe and magical sensory environment for exploration.

⌨️ Keyboard Play Magic

Your toddler can play using almost any key on the keyboard! Pressing alphabet keys, number keys, space, enter, and punctuation triggers tailored visual animations and synthesizer sounds for the active play world:

  • 🎈 Balloon Pop: Press any key to pop floating balloons one by one! Very keyboard-friendly!
  • 🐶 Animal World: Pressing 1-9 or animal letters (like D for Dog, C for Cat, M for Monkey) triggers their bouncy animations and sounds!
  • 🎨 Magic Paint: Stamps giant glowing neon letters/digits onto the canvas!
  • 🎹 Music World: Play real pentatonic scale notes using keys 1-8, A-K, or Z-,. Cycle instruments with I or Space!
  • 🎆 Fireworks: Launches rockets that explode into colorful text showers of the pressed key!
  • 🚀 Space World: Shoots glowing key comets across the sky and activates thrusters!

Note: Standard system keys (Esc, Tab, Ctrl, Shift, Alt, Cmd/Win, and Function keys F1-F12) are bypassed to keep the browser functioning normally.

📸 Customizing Photos

You can add your own family photos to float in balloons, confetti, and puzzle games:

  1. Create or open the photos/ folder in the project directory.
  2. Paste your toddler's favorite images (JPG, PNG, WEBP, GIF).
  3. Right-click generate-photos.ps1 and select Run with PowerShell to register your photos in the game.

🔒 Parent Settings Panel

To access volume levels, theme presets, playlist controls, and the child lock:

Long-press the top-left corner of the screen for 5 seconds, then solve the math security quiz to unlock.

📱 Play Offline (PWA)

MuddyBoard is a Progressive Web App. You can install it on your iPad, tablet, or phone (via "Add to Home Screen" or "Install App") and play fully offline anywhere without internet.
